# OAuth

We support several enabled OAuth logins to suit users signing in to your DApp. Here is a list of them :   
1. Google \(try Torus on [https://ddex.io/](https://ddex.io/)\)  
2. Facebook   
3. Reddit   
4. Twitch   
5. Discord
6. Torus Email and Password Login
